Deep Dive

A closer look:
Path in Motion.

The Problem

Early-career professionals — especially first-gen students and career changers — have no single platform that brings together job discovery, mentorship access, and scholarship information. Job boards feel overwhelming. Mentorship is inaccessible. There's no personalization.

My Solution

A mobile-first web app with AI-powered matching that surfaces personalized opportunities and connects users to mentors in their field — all wrapped in an interface that feels as intuitive as the apps they already love.

My Role

I led product design and frontend development end-to-end — defining the information architecture, designing the UI in Figma with an iOS-inspired aesthetic, and building the frontend with React + Vite + Tailwind CSS.
